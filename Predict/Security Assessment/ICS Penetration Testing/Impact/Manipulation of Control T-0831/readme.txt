
ID:		T0831
Tactic:		Impact

Adversaries may manipulate physical process control within the industrial environment. 
Methods of manipulating control can include changes to set point values, tags, or other 
parameters. Adversaries may manipulate control systems devices or possibly leverage 
their own, to communicate with and command physical control processes. The duration of 
manipulation may be temporary or longer sustained, depending on operator detection.

Methods of Manipulation of Control include:
Man-in-the-middle
Spoof command message
Changing setpoints
