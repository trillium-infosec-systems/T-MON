	Computer Virus
A type of malware, they are unique pieces of code that can wreak havoc 
and spread from computer to computer. Malware infections are quite common, 
and a virus-like Trojan horse can severely damage a system network. If you 
click on an email with a malicious link or download links from infected 
websites, these viruses can corrupt your files, infect other computers 
from your list and steal your personal information.
