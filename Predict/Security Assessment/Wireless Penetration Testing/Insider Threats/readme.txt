	Insider Threat
An insider threat is a security risk that originates from within the
targeted organization. It typically involves a current or former 
employee or business associate who has access to sensitive information 
or privileged accounts within the network of an organization, and who 
misuses this access. someone who maliciously and intentionally abuses 
legitimate credentials, typically to steal information for financial 
or personal incentives.
